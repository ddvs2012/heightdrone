About Another

Another is a design studio focused on the middle way that joins digital and analog, art and commerce, reach and resonance. We carefully design websites, publications, brands, and systems in the belief that little details make a big difference.

About Frank Chimero

Frank is the creative director and design grunt at Another. His work has been recognized by Art Directors Club, Print Magazine, Communication Arts, Monocle, and more. In 2011, Frank was honored by Print Magazine as a New Visual Artist (30 under 30) and the ADC as a Young Gun. He frequently speaks at conferences and schools as an authority on design theory and practice.
